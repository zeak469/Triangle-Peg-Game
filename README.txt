How to play: https://www.wikihow.com/Win-the-Peg-Game

At the start of the game, you will be asked to remove a peg, tap to remove a peg. Then, tap an active, blue peg, to see where the peg can jump to. The peg can jump over other pegs to remove them, similar to checkers. Your goal is to have 1 peg remaining.


Once you win, you will be asked for your initials, and it will store your time in a high scores NSObject.

Game must be played in horizontal mode
